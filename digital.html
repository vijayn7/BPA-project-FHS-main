Why is it good for the environment?
Going digital in the workplace and at home can be an easy and beneficial way to reduce your carbon footprint. Going digital eliminates the necessity for standard stationery such as notebooks and pencils, commodities that need the energy to be produced. This energy comes in the form of burning fossil fuels, which releases carbon into the atmosphere. Reducing our dependence on these stationery products and other manufactured goods and transferring data online can significantly impact our carbon footprint.

How does it benefit the consumer?
Switching from traditional stationery to online can offer some benefits to people. For one, all of your data and information is stored in one place, and it is no longer a necessity to search for documents. Along with this, you can work collaboratively with peers or colleagues on documents online, making it easier than ever to share ideas. Simply digitizing information and data can create a better work experience for you and reduce your carbon footprint.
