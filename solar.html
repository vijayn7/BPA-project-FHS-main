Solar panels are one of the most efficient ways to reduce CO2 emissions in your community. Burning fossil fuels, which is the most popular method of creating energy, accounts for 86% of energy imports and 69% of energy exports. Fossil fuels are harmful to the environment because they release toxic levels of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gasses into the atmosphere. In a 2019 study, it was reported that fossil fuel consumption accounted for 74% of all GHG emissions in the U.S. Making a small change to have solar panels installed can help combat this problem as it lessens dependency on fossil fuels in turn lowering GHG emissions.

How can solar panels be beneficial for the average customer?
Solar panels can act as a cost effective solution to paying your energy bills. Solar panels can generate electricity that can offset the cost of your energy bill, making it a cost efficient solution to reducing expenses each month.
