How is it good for the environment?
Using LED light bulbs is a straightforward way to help reduce your carbon footprint. LED bulbs help reduce carbon emissions by drawing less energy. According to the department of energy, LED bulbs use 75% less energy than a traditional incandescent, meaning that less CO2, a byproduct of electricity, is released into the atmosphere. All of this comes without even looking at the other benefits of LEDs such as less toxic materials and longer usage time. Just deciding to change incandescent bulbs to LEDs can greatly make an impact on your carbon footprint.

How is it beneficial to the consumer?
Using LEDs is beneficial to the consumer because it helps you save on your energy bill. LEDs require less power draw, meaning that they will help reduce the amount you pay for energy monthly.
